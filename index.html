My Story

Hi, my name's Shay & I'm a London based artist. Like many others, during the Covid lockdowns, I longed to break out of the isolation prison and turned to painting as a form of escapism. I found learning to paint really helped me appreciate the beauty in my surroundings - especially on those permitted, daily walks to the grocery store. I'd love to continue developing my art style and hope some of these pieces can inspire the creativity in you too!
